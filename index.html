Our Causes

We champion education, health, and safety, empowering women and children to overcome challenges and build brighter, self-reliant futures together.

Education for Girls

Empowering girls through quality education, breaking barriers, and opening doors to brighter, independent futures.

Women's Skill Development

Providing skill training for women to achieve financial independence, confidence, and improved livelihoods for their families.

Health & Nutrition

Ensuring women and children receive essential healthcare, nutrition, and hygiene support for healthy, thriving lives.

Safety & Protection

Protecting women and children from abuse, trafficking, and violence, offering safe shelter and legal assistance.

Menstrual Hygiene Awareness

Promoting menstrual health education and access to sanitary products, empowering girls with dignity and confidence.

Child Rights & MEBSS

Defending children's rights to safety, education, and care, nurturing their growth in safe, supportive environments.

Our Latest Events

EmpowerHer Sports Day

A fun-filled day of team sports and games designed to boost confidence, teamwork, and physical health among girls and women. Participants enjoy friendly competitions in activities like relay races, football, and volleyball, promoting fitness and empowerment.

Stitching Futures: Sewing Skills Workshop

A hands-on sewing event where women and older girls learn tailoring basics, fabric selection, and creative stitching. The workshop empowers participants with practical skills for self-reliance and opens opportunities for income generation.

Wellness for All: Community Yoga Morning

A group yoga session led by certified instructors, focusing on physical wellness, stress relief, and mindfulness. Open to women and children of all ages, this event encourages healthy habits and emotional balance in a supportive environment.
